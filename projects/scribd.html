Description:

Welcome to the **Scribd Downloader Bot**! This bot allows you to easily download documents, presentations, and other content from Scribd.

How to Use:

1. Simply send the **Scribd document URL** to the bot to get a direct download link.
2. Supported Scribd URLs include:

  • Documents: https://www.scribd.com/document/...
  • Docs: https://www.scribd.com/doc/...
  • Presentations: https://www.scribd.com/presentation/...

Once the link is received, the bot will generate a direct link for downloading the content.
