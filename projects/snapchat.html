Description:

Welcome to the **Snapchat Downloader Bot**! This bot allows you to effortlessly download content from Snapchat, including:

  • Snapchat Spotlight stories
  • Snapchat posts and stories
  • Public Snapchat profiles

Simply provide the Snapchat content URL or the username of the public profile (without the @) to receive direct download links.

How to Use:

1. Send a **Snapchat Spotlight URL** for story downloads.
2. Share a **public Snapchat profile URL** for profile content.
3. Alternatively, send a **Snapchat username** (without the @) for retrieving public stories and posts.
